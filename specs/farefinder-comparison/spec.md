# FareFinder Comparison Specification

## Goal

Users can compare estimated ride fares and shopping results from one interface
before opening the original provider platform.

## Functional Requirements

- Users can sign up and log in.
- Users can enter pickup and destination locations.
- Users can filter ride results by transport type.
- Users can compare product results by name or uploaded photo keywords.
- Results show provider, estimated price, rating or timing details, and a link
  to the original platform.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- The app must avoid committing secrets.
- The app must document setup, usage, security, and contribution workflows.
- The project must remain licensed under AGPLv3.
