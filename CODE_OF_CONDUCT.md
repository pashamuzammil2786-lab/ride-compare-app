# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and considerate language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Accept responsibility and correct mistakes quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, commits, or merge requests that violate
these standards.
